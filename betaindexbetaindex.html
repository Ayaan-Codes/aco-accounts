The Rocket Science

End-to-End Encryption

Encrypted email and attachments up to 50MB, both using AES 256 GCM. Your password is never sent over the network.

Safety and Speed

Decentralized storage system enables you to own your data. With end-to-end encryption this makes your emails and attachments resistant to tampering, censorship and data failures.

Easy to Use

All familiar web interface allows day-to-day interactions with your email, seamless sending of encrypted email as well as non-encrypted email.

Performance

Supports multi-threaded encryption and upload of larger attachments. Fast retrieval of data from decentralized network.
