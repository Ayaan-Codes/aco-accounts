About

ACO accounts is a project by ACO to make an accounts system. This system will be one of the most easy to use and flexible systems to date.

ACO Accounts

The most easy to use, computer frienly, and connectable accounts system you can find!

Connecting

Your ACO Account can be linked to another account to chat, sahre documents, or do anything else you may want to do.

MyHub

Each user will have a MyHub, this is where you will see a dashboard for your account and be able to quickly see what apps you use and navigate to them.

Development

ACO Accounts plans to be the most secure and least computer intensive accounts system. We will be using our back end coding language ACOde to do all the work server side so your computer has to do less.

Your Account

Your account wll consist of your user name and a 5 diget number at the end, you can change that number with ACO prime.

Ex: WillToadSmith#00000
